Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

Is my clipboard data secure?

Absolutely! All your clipboard data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage API. Nothing is ever sent to external servers. Your data stays private and under your control.

Does ClipStash work offline?

Yes! ClipStash works completely offline. Since all data is stored locally, you can access your clipboard history anytime, even without an internet connection.

What browsers are supported?

ClipStash works with all Chromium-based browsers including Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, Brave, Arc, Opera, and Vivaldi.

How many clips can I store?

ClipStash stores up to 100 clips by default. Pinned items don't count toward this limit and are kept indefinitely. Older unpinned clips are automatically removed when the limit is reached.

Is ClipStash really free?

Yes, ClipStash is completely free and open source. There are no premium tiers, no subscriptions, and no hidden costs. We believe everyone deserves a great clipboard manager.

Can I sync clips across devices?

Currently, ClipStash stores data locally for maximum privacy. Cross-device sync is on our roadmap as an optional feature for users who want it.
